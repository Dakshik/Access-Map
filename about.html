Who We Are

Meet the founders who are making navigation accessible for everyone.

Dakshi Kaushik

Developer

I created this idea because I saw how difficult it is for individuals in wheelchairs to navigate cities due to inaccessible routes, blocked ramps, and limited real-time information. We wanted to build a tool that empowers people with disabilities by providing wheelchair-friendly navigation, real-time obstacle reporting, and community-driven accessibility insights to make mobility easier and more independent.

Rishi Patel

Developer

By developing AccessMap, we aim to provide real-time, reliable information on wheelchair-friendly paths, helping individuals move freely and confidently without unnecessary barriers.

Our Mission

At AccessMap, we believe that everyone deserves the freedom to navigate their world independently and confidently. Our mission is to create a comprehensive platform that provides accurate, up-to-date information about wheelchair-accessible routes and locations, empowering individuals to explore their communities without barriers.
